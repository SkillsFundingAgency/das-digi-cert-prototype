Check you’ve proved your identity

We need to check if you’ve proved your identity with GOV.UK One Login to give you access to your certificates and keep them safe.

If you’ve already proved your identity, you can check your details on the next page.

To prove your identity GOV.UK will ask you some questions and you’ll need:

  • your passport
  • your driving licence

or

  • an equivalent photo ID

Users without photo ID can instead use their UK bank account details and HMRC tax record to prove their identity.

You can learn more about how users can prove their identity.
