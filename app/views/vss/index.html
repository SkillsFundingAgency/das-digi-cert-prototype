Recruit apprentices who may have been made redundant

Apprentices who have been made redundant may already have the knowledge, skills and behaviours to add value to your organisation. These apprentices can:

  • fill key skill gaps in your organisation or your existing apprenticeship vacancies
  • continue with their apprenticeship and build on their existing skills

To recruit apprentices, you’ll need to create an apprenticeship service account.

Before you start you'll need:

  • an email address
  • permission to add PAYE schemes to the account
  • permission to accept the employer agreement on behalf of your organisation

You'll also need either the Government Gateway login for your organisation, or your accounts office reference number and employer PAYE scheme reference number.
