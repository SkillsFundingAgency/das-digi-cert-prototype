Add a PAYE scheme

We need you to add the PAYE scheme for your organisation. You can add your PAYE scheme by using:

  • the Government Gateway login for your organisation or
  • your Accounts Office reference and Employer PAYE reference
How to find your Government Gateway details
If you have lost your Government Gateway log in details you can get help signing in
How to find your Accounts Office reference and Employer PAYE reference

You can find your 13-character Accounts Office reference on the letter HMRC sent you when you first registered as an employer.

You will find your Employer PAYE reference in the payslip booklet HMRC provided. Contact HMRC or your accountant if you do not have access to the booklet.
