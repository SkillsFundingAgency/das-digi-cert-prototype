Add PAYE scheme

We need you to add the PAYE scheme for your organisation.

If your organisation payroll is under £3 million you can use:

  • the Government Gateway login for your organisation, or
  • the accounts office reference number (AORN) for your organisation
Find your Government Gateway details
If you have lost your Government Gateway log in details you can get help signing in
Find your accounts office and PAYE scheme reference numbers
You can find your 13-character accounts office reference numbers on the letter HMRC sent you when you first registered as an employer. You will find your employer PAYE scheme reference in the payslip booklet HMRC provided. Contact HMRC or your accountant if you do not have access to the booklet.

You can add a PAYE scheme later if you need more time, we can still create your account for you.
